Eco-Friendly Practices 🌎

  • Reduce. Reuse. Recycle. Upcycle.
  • Ride a bike more and drive less.
  • Carry your own bowls and utensils to avoid using disposable ones.
  • Install solar panels on your roof.
  • Carry your eco bag and waterbottle around.
  • Thrift more and buy less fast fashion.
  • Unplug electrical devices when you are not using them.
  • Use plant-made or recycled toothbrushes.
  • Have less meat.
  • ...

More sustainable lifestyle for a healthier earth and a better future of the humankind.
